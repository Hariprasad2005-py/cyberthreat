The Evolving Threat Landscape

In today's hyper-connected world, traditional rule-based firewalls and IDS are no longer sufficient. Modern cyber-attacks are polymorphic, stealthy, and move at speeds that outpace human intervention.

  • Over 30,000 websites are hacked daily across the globe.
  • Ransomware attacks happen every 11 seconds.
  • Standard detection systems generate 40% false positives.

Why AI?

"To fight a machine-led attack, you need machine-led defense."

Our system leverages machine learning models that learn the 'baseline' of normal network behavior. This allows for the detection of zero-day vulnerabilities and subtle anomalies that signature-based systems completely miss.

Project Abstract

This project proposes an intelligent cybersecurity ecosystem that utilizes a dual AI architecture — Random Forest Classifier (97% accuracy) and Isolation Forest — to monitor network traffic in real-time. By extracting high-dimensional patterns from 26 CICIDS2017 packet features, our system identifies malicious intent ranging from DDoS attacks to lateral movement. A trend-weighted prediction algorithm forecasts the next likely threat type with a confidence score. The integration of Network Anomaly Intelligence ensures that even the most subtle deviations from normal operational metrics are flagged, visualized, and mitigated instantly through an interactive dashboard.
